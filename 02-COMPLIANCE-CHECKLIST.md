| "Eliminates" | "Helps address" |
| "100% effective" | "High efficacy rates in studies" |
| "No side effects" | "Generally well-tolerated" |

| "Prescription not needed" | "Prescribed after consultation" |

## Content Type-Specific Rules

### Blog Posts
- Medical disclaimer in footer or sidebar
- Sources linked or cited
- "Not medical advice" language somewhere
- Provider consultation CTA

### Social Media
- Claims must be supportable even without full context
- No before/after photos without disclaimer
- Link to full information where possible
- Platform-specific ad policies apply

### Email
- Unsubscribe option
- Clear sender identification
- No misleading subject lines
- Same claims standards as other content

### Video/TikTok
- Verbal disclaimers or text overlay for claims
- "Not medical advice" in description
- No white coat unless actual provider
- Avoid "doctor" title unless verified

## Standard Disclaimers

### General Medical Disclaimer
```
The information provided is for educational purposes only and is not intended as medical advice. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider before starting any treatment. Individual results may vary.
```

### Treatment-Specific Disclaimer
```
[Treatment name] is a prescription medication that requires evaluation by a licensed provider. Not everyone qualifies. Side effects may occur. Results vary by individual.
```

### Cannabis Content Disclaimer
```
Cannabis laws vary by state. This content is for informational purposes about policy changes and does not constitute medical advice or encouragement to use cannabis. Consult local laws and a healthcare provider.
```

## Escalation

**If unsure about a claim:**
1. Default to more conservative language
2. Add citation if claim is important
3. Flag for legal/compliance review if high-risk topic
4. When in doubt, cut it out

**High-risk topics requiring extra review:**
- Controlled substances
- Mental health claims
- Pediatric anything
- Pregnancy/fertility
- Cancer (never claim treatment)
- Specific disease cure claims
