## Voice & Tone
- Professional but approachable — not clinical or cold
- Science-informed — reference studies and mechanisms, but explain them simply
- Empowering — help patients feel in control of their health journey
- Trustworthy — no hype, no miracle claims

## Copy Guidelines
- Use "you/your" to address the reader directly
- Lead with the problem/frustration, then introduce the solution
- Avoid: "cure," "treat," "guaranteed results," "miracle"
- Prefer: "may support," "clinically studied," "designed to help"
- Always include a human element — mention the provider consultation

## Compliance Requirements (CRITICAL)
- FDA supplement disclaimer: "These statements have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration. This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease."
- Include "Consult your healthcare provider before starting any supplement"
- No before/after photos paired with specific medical claims
- Telehealth disclaimer: "Services provided by licensed healthcare providers"
- Privacy notice link required on all pages
- HIPAA-compliant language — never imply access to patient records

## Conversion Elements
- Primary CTA: "Start Your Consultation" / "Get Started Today"
- Trust signals: Licensed providers, telehealth certifications, patient count
- Social proof: Patient testimonials (with disclaimer that results may vary)
- Urgency: Limited-time pricing, availability windows
