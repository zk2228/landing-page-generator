# Wild Bill's Tobacco — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Industry:** Tobacco retail franchise
- **Tagline:** "Your Neighborhood Smoke Shop, Since 1992."
- **Mission:** Be the most trusted and welcoming tobacco retail destination in every community we serve, with a focus on Nashville expansion.

## Visual Identity

### Colors
- **Primary:** #8B2F0A (Deep Rust Red — heritage, warmth, established feel)
- **Secondary:** #2C2C2C (Charcoal — grounded, reliable)
- **Accent:** #D4A24E (Mustard Gold — approachable, inviting)
- **Background:** #FBF8F3 (Warm Cream)
- **Surface:** #F0EBE3 (Light Tan — card backgrounds)
- **Text Primary:** #2C2C2C (Charcoal)
- **Text Secondary:** #6B6358 (Warm Gray)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Playfair Display (Bold, 700 weight)
- **Body:** Source Sans 3 (Regular, 400 weight)
- **Accent/CTAs:** Source Sans 3 (Semi-Bold, 600 weight)

### Logo Usage
- Full logo preferred on light backgrounds
- Mark-only version acceptable for small spaces (min 40px)
- Always include ™ designation
- Do not place on backgrounds that reduce contrast below WCAG AA

## Tone of Voice
- **Approachable** — like talking to a knowledgeable friend
- **Community-oriented** — emphasize local presence and neighborhood roots
- **Established** — reference the brand's 30+ year history
- **Knowledgeable** — staff are experts who can guide your choices
- **Welcoming** — everyone (of legal age) is welcome

### Do:
- "Stop by your neighborhood Wild Bill's"
- "Our tobacconists have been helping customers find their perfect smoke for over 30 years"
- "Now open in Nashville — come see what everyone's talking about"

### Don't:
- Health benefit claims about any tobacco product
- Language targeting anyone under 21
- Disparaging other brands or competitors
- "Cheap" — use "value" or "everyday low prices" instead

## Compliance Requirements

### Age Gate (Required)
Same requirements as LighterUSA:
- Must appear before any product content is visible
- Prompt: "Are you 21 or older?"
- Options: "Yes, I'm 21+" (proceeds to page) / "No" (redirects away)
- Must store verification in session/cookie

### Nicotine Warning (Required on Every Page)
Include the following warning prominently:
> **WARNING:** This product contains nicotine. Nicotine is an addictive chemical.

- Must be visible without scrolling OR in a persistent banner

### Tobacco Advertising Restrictions
- No health benefit claims for any tobacco products
- No imagery or language appealing to minors
- No free sample promotions
- No claims that products are "safe" or "harmless"
- No testimonials claiming health improvements

### Location-Specific Requirements (Nashville Expansion)
- Include Nashville store address and hours when generating Nashville-specific pages
- Reference local community involvement
- Include Google Maps embed or link for directions
- Mention "Now Open" or "Coming Soon" as appropriate

## Page Elements

### Required on Every Page
1. Age verification gate (before content)
2. Nicotine warning banner
3. Navigation with logo, store locator link
4. Hero section (location-aware when applicable)
5. Store locations section or link to store finder
6. Footer with legal disclaimers, policy links, and corporate info
7. "Must be 21+ to purchase" in footer

### CTAs (Calls to Action)
- Primary: "Find a Store" / "Visit Us Today" / "See What's New"
- Secondary: "View Products" / "Get Directions"
- Style: Rounded corners (6px), rust red background, warm cream text

### Nashville-Specific Elements
- Grand opening banner (when applicable)
- Local events section
- Nashville store gallery
- Community involvement highlights

## SEO Guidelines
- Target keywords: tobacco shop near me, smoke shop Nashville, Wild Bill's Tobacco, cigar shop, pipe tobacco store
- Meta description max: 160 characters
- Include Open Graph tags with location data
- Local SEO: Include structured data (LocalBusiness schema) for each location
- Google Business Profile integration guidance
