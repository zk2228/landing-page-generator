# RevitalizeMe — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Industry:** Telehealth / Wellness
- **Tagline:** "Your wellness journey, guided by science."
- **Mission:** Make evidence-based wellness accessible through convenient telehealth consultations and curated supplement programs.

## Visual Identity

### Colors
- **Primary:** #2A7D5F (Forest Green — trust, health, growth)
- **Secondary:** #1B4D6E (Deep Teal — professionalism, calm)
- **Accent:** #E8A54B (Warm Gold — warmth, premium feel)
- **Background:** #F9F7F4 (Warm White)
- **Text Primary:** #1A1A2E (Near Black)
- **Text Secondary:** #5A5A6E (Medium Gray)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Inter (Bold, 600/700 weight)
- **Body:** Inter (Regular, 400 weight)
- **Accent/CTAs:** Inter (Semi-Bold, 600 weight)

### Logo Usage
- Minimum clear space: 16px on all sides
- Do not place on busy backgrounds
- Approved colors: Primary green on light backgrounds, white on dark backgrounds

## Tone of Voice
- **Professional** but not clinical
- **Warm** and empathetic — the reader is on a personal journey
- **Trustworthy** — back claims with phrases like "clinician-reviewed," "evidence-informed"
- **Accessible** — avoid dense medical jargon; explain terms when used
- **Empowering** — the patient is in control of their health decisions

### Do:
- "Our board-certified providers work with you to create a personalized plan"
- "Backed by peer-reviewed research"
- "Start your consultation from the comfort of home"

### Don't:
- "Cure your condition now!"
- "Guaranteed results"
- "This supplement will fix your health problems"

## Compliance Requirements

### HIPAA-Aware Marketing
- Never reference specific patient outcomes or testimonials that could identify individuals
- Do not collect PHI (Protected Health Information) through marketing forms
- Contact forms should only collect: name, email, phone, general area of interest
- Always link to the full Privacy Policy

### FDA Disclaimers (Required on Supplement Pages)
Include the following verbatim:
> *These statements have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration. This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.*

### Medical Claims
- NEVER make specific medical claims (e.g., "lowers blood pressure by 20%")
- Use qualifiers: "may support," "designed to help," "consult your healthcare provider"
- ALWAYS include: "Consult your healthcare provider before starting any new supplement or wellness program."

### Testimonials
- Must include: "Individual results may vary"
- No before/after photos paired with medical claims
- All testimonials must be from verified customers

## Page Elements

### Required on Every Page
1. Navigation with logo
2. Hero section with clear value proposition
3. "Consult your healthcare provider" disclaimer in footer
4. Privacy Policy link
5. Terms of Service link
6. Contact information

### CTAs (Calls to Action)
- Primary: "Start Your Consultation" / "Get Started Today"
- Secondary: "Learn More" / "See How It Works"
- Style: Rounded corners (8px), primary green background, white text

## SEO Guidelines
- Target keywords: telehealth, online wellness, virtual health consultation, personalized wellness
- Meta description max: 160 characters
- Include Open Graph tags for social sharing
