# LighterUSA — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Industry:** E-commerce — Nicotine pouches, vapes, smoking accessories
- **Tagline:** "Elevate Your Experience."
- **Mission:** Provide premium nicotine and smoking products with a modern, curated shopping experience for adult consumers.

## Visual Identity

### Colors
- **Primary:** #FF4D00 (Electric Orange — energy, boldness)
- **Secondary:** #1C1C1E (Near Black — sophistication, modern)
- **Accent:** #00C2FF (Cyan Blue — freshness, tech-forward)
- **Background:** #FFFFFF (White)
- **Surface:** #F5F5F7 (Light Gray — card backgrounds)
- **Text Primary:** #1C1C1E (Near Black)
- **Text Secondary:** #6E6E73 (Medium Gray)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Space Grotesk (Bold, 700 weight)
- **Body:** DM Sans (Regular, 400 weight)
- **Accent/CTAs:** Space Grotesk (Semi-Bold, 600 weight)

### Logo Usage
- Always display on solid backgrounds (white or near-black)
- Minimum size: 120px wide
- Do not distort, rotate, or add effects

## Tone of Voice
- **Bold** and confident — no hedging
- **Modern** — speaks to a lifestyle-conscious adult audience
- **Direct** — short sentences, punchy copy
- **Lifestyle-focused** — products are part of a curated personal experience
- **Never preachy** — respects the consumer's choices

### Do:
- "Premium pouches, delivered to your door"
- "Crafted for those who know what they want"
- "Explore our curated collection"

### Don't:
- "Smoking is cool" or any glorification targeting youth
- "Healthier alternative" or any health benefit claims
- "Try your first vape" or any language suggesting new users

## Compliance Requirements

### Age Gate (Required)
Every landing page MUST include an age verification gate:
- Must appear before any product content is visible
- Prompt: "Are you 21 or older?"
- Options: "Yes, I'm 21+" (proceeds to page) / "No" (redirects away)
- Must store verification in session/cookie
- Do not use date-of-birth fields (not sufficient for compliance)

### Nicotine Warning (Required on Every Page)
Include the following warning prominently on every page:
> **WARNING:** This product contains nicotine. Nicotine is an addictive chemical.

- Must be visible without scrolling OR in a persistent banner
- Minimum font size: 14px
- Must have sufficient contrast (WCAG AA minimum)

### Advertising Restrictions
- No claims that products are "safe," "healthy," or "harmless"
- No comparison to FDA-approved cessation products
- No testimonials claiming health improvements
- No imagery or language that could appeal to minors (no cartoons, candy references, etc.)
- No free sample promotions

### Shipping & Legal
- Include: "Must be 21+ to purchase. ID verification required at delivery."
- Link to Terms of Service and Shipping Policy

## Page Elements

### Required on Every Page
1. Age verification gate (before content)
2. Nicotine warning banner (persistent or above fold)
3. Navigation with logo and cart
4. Hero section with featured products/promotions
5. Footer with legal disclaimers, shipping info, and policy links
6. "Must be 21+ to purchase" in footer

### CTAs (Calls to Action)
- Primary: "Shop Now" / "Add to Cart" / "Browse Collection"
- Secondary: "View Details" / "See All Products"
- Style: Sharp corners (4px), electric orange background, white text, bold font

## SEO Guidelines
- Target keywords: nicotine pouches online, buy vapes online, smoking accessories, premium vape shop
- Meta description max: 160 characters
- Include Open Graph tags for social sharing
- Canonical URLs for product pages
