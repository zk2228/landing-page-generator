# Twitter/X Platform Playbook

Complete guide to creating content for Twitter/X. Use alongside the Social Content Playbook.

---

## Platform Overview

**Best for:** Real-time conversation, thought leadership, news commentary, educational threads, community building

**Audience:** Skews professional, tech-savvy, news-interested. Wide age range but engagement heaviest 25-45.

**Algorithm priorities:**
- Replies and conversation
- Retweets with comments
- Time spent reading
- Profile visits after viewing
- Early engagement velocity

**Posting frequency:** 3-7 tweets/day for active accounts. Minimum 1/day. Threads count as one "post" but multiple impressions.

### 3. Tweet + Image

**Best for:** Quote cards, data visualization, screenshots, memes

**Image specs:**
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 (1200x675) or 1:1 (1200x1200)
- Text must be large and readable on mobile
- Brand colors for consistency
- Minimal text â€” tweet does the heavy lifting

### 4. Tweet + Video

**Best for:** Tutorials, reactions, clips, behind-the-scenes

**Video specs:**
- Max length: 2:20 (most videos should be 30-90 seconds)
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 1:1
- Captions essential (most watch muted)
- Hook in first 3 seconds

### 5. Quote Tweet

**Best for:** Commentary, adding context, respectful disagreement, amplifying others

**Structure:**
```

```

**Etiquette:**
- Add genuine value, not just "This ðŸ‘†"
- Don't dunk without substance

### 6. Reply/Thread Continuation

**Best for:** Engagement, conversation, adding context to your posts

**Strategy:**
- Reply to your own tweets with additional thoughts
- Reply early to others' posts in your niche
- Provide value, not just agreement

## Engagement Strategy for Twitter

### Posting Rhythm

**Optimal times:** 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-7 PM (in your audience's timezone)

**Weekly rhythm:**
- Monday: Strong educational thread (people catching up)
- Tuesday-Thursday: Mix of threads, singles, engagement
- Friday: Lighter content, questions, engagement
- Weekend: Optional â€” engagement-focused if posting

### The First 30 Minutes

Algorithm rewards early engagement. After posting:
1. Stay online for at least 30 minutes
2. Reply to every comment immediately
3. Like relevant tweets in your niche
4. Don't post and disappear

### Building Through Replies

Replying to larger accounts is the fastest growth hack:
- Reply early (within first 30 min of their post)
- Add genuine value (not "Great point!")
- Make your reply standalone valuable
- Be consistent â€” same accounts, same niches

### Twitter Etiquette

**Do:**
- Give credit when sharing others' ideas
- Quote tweet instead of copying
- Engage with your replies
- Support others in your niche

**Don't:**
- Steal content without attribution
- Reply-spam larger accounts
- Beg for follows/RTs
- Post engagement bait without value

|--------|-----------------|
| Impressions | How many people saw it |
| Engagements | Total interactions |
| Engagement rate | Engagements / Impressions |
| Profile visits | Interest generated |
| Link clicks | Action taken |

| Follower Count | Good ER | Great ER |
|----------------|---------|----------|

| 10K-100K | 1-2% | 2-4%+ |

### Thread-Specific Metrics

- First tweet impressions vs. last tweet impressions (thread retention)

## Twitter for Regulated Industries

### Healthcare/Telehealth
- No diagnosis language even in casual tweets
- Stats need to be accurate
- "May help" not "will cure"
- Testimonial tweets need context

### Cannabis/Nicotine/Age-Restricted
- Twitter is relatively permissive but screenshot risk
- No targeting minors
- Don't show consumption
- Be careful with claims

### Financial
- Disclaimers can be in thread, not every tweet
- No guaranteed returns
- Proper disclosures in bio/thread
